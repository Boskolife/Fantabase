Our Solution:
AI-Powered Feed & Assistant

Unified, personalized feed

Fantabase syncs with your fantasy leagues (one-click Sleeper integration or manual entry) and automatically builds a curated stream of “actionable intelligence” for the players you actually own. Only relevant news, analysis and data appear in your feed – no fluff.

Roster-focused updates

We aggregate all sources (social media, expert blogs, local beat reporters, YouTube сlips) and filter them down to what impacts your lineup. In effect, “Your feed = your lineup’s pulse” – nothing extra, nothing missed.

Smart watchlists & real-time alerts

Add any player (including players you don’t own) to a custom watchlist. When a tracked player has news (e.g. injury, inactive, trade), Fantabase sends a push notification immediately . You’ll know before your league does, giving you first-mover advantage.

AI Chat Assistant (“Howie Wynn”)

Our assistant handles the endless lineup questions (waiver targets, injury impact, start/sit decisions, trade advice, projections, etc.) in seconds. Howie Wynn has been “trained” on a massive library of fantasy analysis – effectively a 24/7 personal scouting team.

Powerful data engine

Under the hood we use SportsDataIO’s APIs (an award-winning sports data provider). This supplies real-time stats, news and analytics so our AI and alerts are always up-to-date.
